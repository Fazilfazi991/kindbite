# SvasthaBox (Working Name)
## Personalized Wellness Gifting & Nutrition Platform

> **Tagline:** Send health, care, and nutrition to the people you love.

---

# 1. Vision

SvasthaBox is a personalized wellness-commerce platform that helps people, especially NRIs in GCC countries, send curated nutrition boxes to parents, spouses, children, and loved ones in India.

We are not selling dry fruits.

We are selling:

- Care
- Wellness
- Emotional connection
- Personalized nutrition experiences

---

# 2. Core Positioning

## Primary Positioning

**"Send wellness home."**

A son in Dubai can send a Heart Care Box to his father.

A husband in Qatar can subscribe to a Pregnancy Nutrition Box.

A daughter in Saudi can gift a Diabetes-Friendly Wellness Pack to her mother.
